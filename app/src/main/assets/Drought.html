Drought

A drought is a prolonged period with less than average amounts of rain or snow in a particular region. The severity of the drought depends on the amount of time that a region receives below average precipitation.


Cause of drought

A drought is caused by drier than normal conditions that can eventually lead to water supply problems. Really hot temperatures can make a drought worse by causing moisture to evaporate from the soil.

Diseases occur during drought

  • Severe drought conditions can negatively affect air quality. 
  • During drought, there is an increases risk for wildfires and dust storms. 
  • Particulate matter suspended in the air form these events can irritate the bronchial passages and lungs.
  •  This can make chronic respiratory illnesses worse and increase the risk for respiratory infections like bronchitis and pneumonia.

Prevention

  • Do not cut trees and forests.
  • Do not mess with traditional water sources such as ponds, annicuts, well, tanks, etc.
  • Avoid letting the water run while brushing your teeth, washing your face or shaving.
  • Avoid taking baths. Take shorts showers.
  • Avoid flushing the toilet unnecessarily.
  • Avoid over-fertilizing your lawn.

Preparedness

  • Check sprinkel systems and timing devices regularly to be sure they operate properly.
  • Listen to radio, watch TV and read newspapers for warnings, updates and instructions.
  • Practice rain water harvesting.
  • Place a bucket in the shower to catch excess water for watering plants.
  • Cover pools and spas to reduce evaporation of water.
  • Put a layer of mulch around trees and plants to reduce evaporation and keep the soil cool.

Recovery

  • Use wet clothes to clean and scrub floors instead of using running water.
  • Regularly check tanks, taps, etc to prevent leakage.
  • Choose water efficient irrigation system such as drip irrigation for your trees, shrubs and flowers.
  • Replace your showerhead with an ultra low flow version.
  • For toilets with larger water tanks, install a toilet displacement device to reduce the amount of water needed to flush.
  • Choose appliances that are more water and energy efficient.
